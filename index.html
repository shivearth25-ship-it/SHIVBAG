Our Heritage & Craft

Pioneers in Wholesale Packaging Solutions

For over a decade, Shiv Enterprise has been the trusted manufacturing partner for luxury retail stores, premium boutique brands, and fast-growing e-commerce businesses.

We specialize in custom manufacturing of premium high-gloss black packaging bags. Our signature glossy black bags are engineered with multi-layer high-density polymers, offering an exquisite mirror-like shine alongside industry-leading tear resistance and load-bearing capacity.
